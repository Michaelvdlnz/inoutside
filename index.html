Residential Painting:

Beautify

A beautifully painted house or bedroom is a more inviting place to live and relax. Colours evoke various emotions and create atmosphere within and around your home. A striking feature wall, accents and highlights, smooth and textured surfaces all enhance and create the essence of your home.

Protect

Good exterior paint, including UV protection, is essential to ensure your home stays sound and dry, giving years of protection for your most valuable asset. A well sealed roof, and correct choices for bathroom surfaces along with quality sealants on all moisture-exposed joins in and around your home, ensure your home remains dry and provides the healthiest possible environment for your family.

Eco-Paints / Enhance function

We are able to apply environmentally friendly paints such as low-VOC exposing paints and paints made from recycled ingredients.

We ensure interior surfaces are easily cleaned and resistant to heavy traffic, knocks and scrapes.

Plastering

We offer high-quality gib-sealing services using the correct plaster to ensure perfect finished wall surfaces. We can remediate poorly plastered joins and create a new seamless surface.

Commercial Painting

We have the right tools and project management skills to paint large areas following all health and safety procedures, and within the contracted material and time requirements.

Industrial Painting

For serious protection, various industrial coatings tailored to your premises can be painted or sprayed on to exterior surfaces. This may vary from 2-pot alkyds to water-based high builds depending on your requirements.

Plastering: Whether it is plastering the gib on an entire new build, a small room or remedial plastering we finish plastered surfaces to grade 5 standard.
